What I Do

Four ways I can help
you build better.

Sustainable Design

From concept to completion, every decision made through an environmental lens. Buildings that breathe, adapt, and endure for generations.

Passive House Planning

Architecture that uses natural forces — sun, wind, earth — to create comfort without the energy bill. Zero compromise on quality of space.

Green Consultation

Whether you're starting from scratch or retrofitting, I guide you through certifications, material choices, and the tradeoffs worth making.

Education & Community

Sustainable architecture shouldn't belong only to those who can afford a consultant. I share what I know — openly, practically, in plain language.
